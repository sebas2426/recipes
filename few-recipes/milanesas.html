Milanesas

Description:

This is a typical South American food that it can be made of meat or chicken

It breads the meal and then fries it

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the two eggs lightly and add the garlic and peppercorn mixture. I usually add a Tbsp. of water to the mortar after grinding the garlic and peppercorns.

  2. In a large dish, spread the bread crumbs mixed with salt, and have another dish ready to place the steaks after breading.

  3. Dip the steaks into the egg mixture.

  4. Using kitchen tongs, place one steak into the crumbs, turn to coat both sides. Patting on lightly to make sure the coating adheres to the steak. Place the already-breaded steak aside on a plate. Repeat this step with the remaining steaks.
