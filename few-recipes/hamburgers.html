Hamburgers

Description:

It is a great food made of two slices of bread, an meat, lettuce, eggs and tomatoes

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Herbs and Sauces

Steps:

  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.

  2. Make four patties.

  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
