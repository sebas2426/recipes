French Fries

Description

A simple recipe that is very delicious, it only made of potatoes, very simple, right?

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Vinegar (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the fries

  2. Keep in water to prevent browning

  3. *OPTIONAL* Cook in vinegar water for more crispiness

  4. Drain and dry

  5. Cooking vessel and oil

  6. Fry

  7. Cool for 30 minutes
